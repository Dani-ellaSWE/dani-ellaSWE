Purpose of the Website

This website was created in 2025 to provide accessible mental health resources and coping strategies. It shares practical techniques to improve wellbeing, alongside information on where to seek urgent help if needed.

The aim is to raise awareness, encourage open conversations, and support young people and communities in understanding mental health.
