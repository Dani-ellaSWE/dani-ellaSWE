Mental Health Awareness & Support

MindBloom Hub was created in 2025 as part of a Year 12 Extended Project Qualification, combining research, advocacy, and digital design to promote mental wellbeing within young people and local communities.

About the Project

My name is Daniella Okoye and this platform was developed to explore the importance of mental health awareness, particularly within diverse and underrepresented communities. Through workshops, outreach initiatives, and youth advocacy projects, the goal is to encourage open conversation and reduce stigma surrounding mental wellbeing.

The website provides structured information, support resources, and practical strategies designed to promote positive mental health.

Website Structure

  • Home: Introduction and overview of the platform.
  • About: Background information and contact details.
  • Services: Workshops and support initiatives.
  • Resources: Helplines, coping strategies and useful links.
  • Mental Health: Educational topics and awareness content.
